Help   HOW CAN I SHARE MY RIDE

Click on the OFFER A RIDE button on home tab and submit the form to share a ride

  HOW CAN I FIND MY RIDE

Click on the FIND A RIDE button on home tab and submit the find the desired ride

  CAN I CHANGE MY PROFILE DETAILS

Goto the sidemenu and click on the PROFILE option to check your profile details and click the top right option to edit the profile

  CAN I SHARE MY TRIP DETAILS

You can share your trip details by visting MY TRIPS page and click on the button provided for each specific trip

  ON WHICH SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS
   CAN I SHARE MY TRIP

You can share your trip on INSTAGRAM, FACEBOOK AND WHATSAPP

  CAN I UPDATE MY TRIP DETAILS

NO, you can't update your trip details.However you can delete your trip on MY TRIPS Page and then recreate your trip on SHARE A RIDE PAGE

  WHAT ARE CHANNELS

CHANNELS are used to send update to ride seekers who are intersted in taking a ride with you. Ride Seekers can also ask their queries regarding to the trip on the channel created by ride owner

  HOW CAN I INFORM THE RIDE OWNER
   THAT I AM INTERSTED IN HIS TRIP

You can simply send a message to the user by clicking on the message button in FIND A RIDE page and accordingly the owner will get updated
OR
You can simply inform the owner by messaging him via his channel

  HOW CAN I PAY THE OWNER

We are working on the payment system as of now you can ask the owner how to send the payment via the channel created by him
